How admin can see the result?

In your admin account, click on active competitions button and open your desired competition window then click on RESULTS button then the result of the competition will be appeared.

How to organize a competition?

Open your admin account click on ADD COMPETITION button, then a competition form will appear fill all the details and submit the form. The competition will be added to active competitions screen.

How to create admin account?

First when you open this application on the top right corner in the menu bar you will got a admin login button,click on that button then you will redirect to admin login page. There is a signup link provided, open that link and fill the form and submit it.

If I forgot my admin account password what can I do?

Open admin login screen then click on forgot password link then new screen will be redirected enter your email and submit it. Then the server will automatically send your password to your email.

How a candidate can register?

On the home screen click on register now button available on the top right corner in the menu bar. Then a candidate registration form will appear, fill all the details with the competition name you want to take part in.

What is the login id and examination password of the candidate?

After succesful submission of candidate registration form the login id will automatically send to the candidate's email account and the examination password is the competition id which will be forwarded by the admin.
